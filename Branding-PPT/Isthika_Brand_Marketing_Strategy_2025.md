# ISTHIKA HOMES
## Brand Marketing Strategy 2025
### Annual Budget: ₹1.2 Crores (₹10 Lakhs/Month)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This comprehensive marketing strategy is designed to elevate Isthika Homes' brand presence to match the market position of Souparnika, our benchmark competitor. With a strategic 12-month investment of ₹10 lakhs per month, we aim to bridge the significant digital gap and establish Isthika as a prominent player in the real estate market.

**Budget Allocation:**
- **Digital Marketing: 70% (₹7 Lakhs/month)**
- **Non-Digital Marketing: 30% (₹3 Lakhs/month)**

## RISK MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### Potential Challenges & Solutions

**1. Low Initial Engagement**
- **Risk:** Slow audience growth in initial months
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive paid promotion, influencer partnerships, contests and giveaways

**2. High Competition**
- **Risk:** Competing against established brands like Souparnika
- **Mitigation:** Unique value proposition highlighting, niche targeting, superior Google ratings leverage

**3. Budget Constraints**
- **Risk:** Insufficient budget for comprehensive coverage
- **Mitigation:** Phased approach, focus on high-ROI channels, continuous optimization

**4. Ad Fatigue**
- **Risk:** Declining performance over time
- **Mitigation:** Creative rotation, A/B testing, fresh content production

**5. Platform Algorithm Changes**
- **Risk:** Social media reach decline
- **Mitigation:** Diversified platform strategy, owned media focus, email list building

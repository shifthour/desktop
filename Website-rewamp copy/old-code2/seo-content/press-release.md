# Press Release: Anahata - Premium Gated Community Launch

## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Ishtika Launches Anahata - A 5-Acre Premium Gated Community in Whitefield's Prime Location**

*440 Luxury Apartments with 80% Open Space and 50+ World-Class Amenities Now Available from ₹89 Lakhs*

**Bangalore, India - August 23, 2024** - Ishtika, a leading real estate developer known for quality construction and timely delivery, today announced the launch of Anahata, a prestigious residential project spanning 5 acres in Soukya Road, Whitefield, Bangalore. This meticulously planned gated community features five magnificent towers housing 440 exclusive families, offering the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and modern living.

### Key Highlights:

**Prime Location Advantage:**
- Strategic location in Whitefield, Bangalore's premier IT corridor
- Excellent connectivity to ITPL, Marathahalli, and Outer Ring Road tech corridor
- Close proximity to top schools, hospitals, and shopping centers
- Easy access to major transportation hubs

**Exceptional Design & Space:**
- 5 acres of prime real estate with 80% open space
- 440 thoughtfully designed apartments across 5 towers
- Spacious 2BHK (1164-1279 sq ft) and 3BHK (1511-1758 sq ft) configurations
- Premium specifications with abundant natural light and cross-ventilation

**World-Class Amenities:**
- 20,000 sq ft clubhouse with comprehensive facilities
- 50+ lifestyle amenities including swimming pool, sports courts, and fitness center
- Landscaped gardens, jogging track, and children's play areas
- 24/7 security with modern surveillance systems

**Investment Advantages:**
- Competitive pricing starting from ₹89 Lakhs for 2BHK apartments
- No Pre-EMI policy until possession
- High appreciation potential in Bangalore's fastest-growing IT corridor
- Backed by Ishtika's reputation for quality and timely delivery

How We Choose Technologies

Technology selection is driven by project needs, not trends.

Project Requirements

We match technologies to your specific needs—scale expectations, integration requirements, team expertise, and timeline constraints.

Long-term Maintainability

We favor technologies with strong community support, good documentation, and proven track records. Your codebase should be maintainable for years.

Client Preferences

If your team has existing expertise or platform preferences, we work within those constraints. We adapt to your environment.

## KEY DELIVERABLES

### Mobile Applications
- iOS native app (Swift/SwiftUI)
- Android native app (Kotlin)
- Web application (React/Next.js)
- All optimized for Indian users and networks

### Core Features
- **Activity Tracking:** Steps, distance, calories
- **Advanced Heart Monitoring:** ECG, HRV, AFIB detection
- **Sleep Tracking:** Sleep stages with respiratory rate
- **Stress Management:** Body stress and strain metrics
- **Recovery Scoring:** Comprehensive recovery analysis
- **Health Metrics:** VO2, SpO2, body temp, BP, diabetes monitoring

### Health Records Management
- Smart cloud storage with auto-parsing of medical documents
- Document management (lab reports, prescriptions, vaccination certificates)
- AI-powered data extraction from medical reports
- Secure data sharing with healthcare providers
- Multi-user family management

### AI/ML Capabilities
- Predictive health analytics
- Prescriptive recommendations
- Risk assessment algorithms
- Anomaly detection in health metrics
- Medical document OCR and parsing

### Integrations
- **5 Fitness Trackers:** Fitbit, Apple Watch, Garmin, Xiaomi, Whoop
- **ABHA:** Ayushman Bharat Health Account (complete integration)
- **NDHM:** National Digital Health Mission (HIP/HIU compliance)
- **Bluetooth:** Direct device connectivity

### Reporting
- On-demand PDF reports in English/Hindi
- Custom report templates
- Exportable health data (PDF, CSV, JSON)
- Real-time dashboards and visualizations

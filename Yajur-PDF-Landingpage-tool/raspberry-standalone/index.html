Business Overview

Raspberry is a curriculum-led, full-stack EdTech solution purpose-built for K-12 education. The platform delivers a connected digital ecosystem that integrates content, pedagogy, assessment, and school operations into a single, seamless experience. Designed for B2B and B2G deployment, Raspberry transforms the educational infrastructure of schools through its proprietary platform and curated content library.

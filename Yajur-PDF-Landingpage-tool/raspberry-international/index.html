Business Overview

Project Raspberry is a curriculum-led, full-stack EdTech solution serving K-12 schools across India and the LatAm Caribbean. The company delivers a proprietary platform combining digital content, assessments, learning management, teacher training, and analytics — all integrated into a single, modular platform. Raspberry replaces the need for multiple fragmented vendors with a unified, government-aligned solution that drives measurable learning outcomes.
